Pediatric Services

Comprehensive child healthcare services for your little one's well-being.

Child Health Consultation

Expert diagnosis and treatment for fever, cold, cough, infections, and common childhood illnesses with gentle, caring attention.

Fever, Cold & Illness Care

Quick diagnosis and effective treatment for seasonal illnesses, viral infections, respiratory troubles, and unexplained fevers in children.

Growth & Development Monitoring

Regular tracking of height, weight, head circumference, and developmental milestones to ensure your child is thriving.

Vaccination Guidance

Complete immunization schedule management, age-appropriate vaccine counseling, and catch-up vaccination plans for children of all ages.

Nutrition & Diet Advice

Personalized nutrition guidance for infants, toddlers, and growing children, including breastfeeding support and weaning advice.

Follow-up Consultations

Regular follow-up visits for ongoing treatment plans, medication adjustments, and monitoring recovery for infants and children.

Online Consultation

Consult with Dr. Fatima from the comfort of your home via video call. Ideal for follow-ups, minor concerns, and parental guidance.

Clinic Visit

In-person appointments at our child-friendly clinic with a warm, comfortable environment designed to put children at ease.

Newborn & Infant Care

Specialized care for newborns and infants, including neonatal check-ups, feeding guidance, and early health assessments.
