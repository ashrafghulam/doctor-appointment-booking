Dr. Tanzeel Fatima

Nurturing Little Lives with Compassion

Dr. Tanzeel Fatima is a passionate pediatrician dedicated to providing comprehensive, evidence-based healthcare for infants, toddlers, and children. With a strong foundation in modern medicine and a heartfelt commitment to child wellness, she focuses on early diagnosis, preventive care, and family-centred treatment.

Her approach combines clinical expertise with warmth and patience, ensuring every child feels comfortable and every parent feels confident. Whether it's a routine vaccination, growth monitoring, or treating a childhood illness — Dr. Fatima treats every patient with the utmost care and attention.
